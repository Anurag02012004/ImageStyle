## What Types of Images to Upload?

### Content Image (What you want to stylize)
- **Best choices:**
  - Clear photographs with good detail
  - Portraits or faces
  - Landscapes or scenery
  - Objects or architecture
  - High-resolution images (but not too large - max 10MB)

- **Avoid:**
  - Already heavily stylized images
  - Very blurry or low-quality images
  - Images with too much text

### Style Image (The artistic style to apply)
- **Best choices:**
  - Famous paintings (Van Gogh, Picasso, Monet, etc.)
  - Artistic textures and patterns
  - Abstract art
  - Watercolor paintings
  - Oil paintings
  - Sketch or drawing styles

##  Tips for Best Results

1. **Image Size:** 
   - Good: 500x500 to 2000x2000 pixels
   - The app automatically resizes larger images to 1024px max

2. **File Format:**
   - Supported: PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP
   - Best quality: PNG
   - Smaller file: JPG

3. **Image Quality:**
   - Use clear, high-quality images
   - Avoid compressed or pixelated images

4. **Style Images:**
   - Bold, distinctive artistic styles work best
   - High contrast style images produce more dramatic results
   - Famous paintings often work very well
